Who Am I?

GRAY / BobaScript

Hi there, I'm GRAY, but you can also call me BobaScript. I'm a student software engineer by day and a MMORRPG addict by night. I love to experiment with new technologies and build cool stuff, just for fun. Whether it's a web app, a game, or a random passion project, I'm usually up to something.

When I'm not coding, you can find me binge-watching anime, playing video games, or reading manga. I'm a huge fan of MMORPGs, especially FFXIV. I've been hooked on them since I discovered Free Realms back in 2009. It was the game that sparked my interest in software development and online communities.

I'm always eager to learn new things and connect with like-minded people. If you share my passion for anime, video games, or software development, feel free to reach out. Many of us from Free Realms are still in touch through Discord!
